Instructions

I neat routine to display the dates, days of a particular month.

Usage: <year> <Month>
Example: 2007 January

Weekday calculation
[[month*26-54]/10 + day + [year-1900] + [year-1900]/4 - 34] MOD 7

SUN=0, MON=1,.. SAT=6
